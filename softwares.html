Softwares

I love creating small apps that I can use on a day-to-day basis.
Most are ridiculously small and do not even feature a GUI,
but here you can find useful apps I've created
which have a GUI for convenience.

Steam Market Calculator

Language: Python

The Steam Market Calculator is a small yet very useful program
with a simple GUI made for calculations in the Steam Market.
I have simplified three of the most common calculations made on the Steam Market.


Image To Text Renderer

Language: Python

The Image To Text Renderer is an app made to transform any image into text.
It resizes any image to any given width and
converts each pixels to a character based on its darkness.
It doesn't feature a working GUI at the moment.
